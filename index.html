I'm software engineer - been it this way most of my life (oh my lord!).

Fun facts about me:

  • I grew up and studied in mid size Russian town what Americans call Siberia and Russians call Far East. It was just 2 hours away from China (frankly even less) and the city is much closer to Begin than Moscow.
  • I studied C++ on my own when I was on my first year in the University (the hardest part was to comprehend for loop - not pointers).
  • Later I learned Java and wrote a chat application (chats were super popular at that time). It didn't got any traction though that was expected, was written using applets and swing UI and it was riddled with exceptions due to calling Swing controls from other threads).
  • In my 20-s my favorite language was Perl (still don't understand people who hate it - I remember one of my coworkers literally had a fit and started to scream at me when I mentioned it).
  • I wouln't say I was ever gifted, but for some reason programming languages excite me. I studied tons of them through my life (though you couldn't say it was hard - in general they all very similar except Prolog - the king of the Wierdos).
