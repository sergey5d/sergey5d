Building Trailvy

16 May 2026 · 4 min · projects

I built Trailvy at some point as a website for trail planning.

The stack, as far as I remember it now, was Scala, Cassandra, and some JavaScript. I think Bootstrap was in there too, though I would not swear to every detail anymore.

It was one of those projects where the technical choices made perfect sense at the time, and then years later you look back and mostly remember the energy around it rather than the architecture diagrams.
